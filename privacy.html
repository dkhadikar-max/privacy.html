Who We Are

Build Your Network ("BYN", "we", "us", "our") is a professional networking platform operated at buildyournetwork.online. We connect professionals who are serious about growing their careers, finding collaborators, and building meaningful relationships.

This Privacy Policy applies to the BYN mobile application (Android and iOS) and the website at buildyournetwork.online. By using any BYN service, you agree to the practices described here.

For any privacy questions, contact us at support@buildyournetwork.online.

What We Collect

Information you provide directly

Data Type Examples Required?
Account details Full name, email address, password (hashed) Yes
Profile information Bio, skills, career intent, interests, profile photo, current role, company Optional
Location City/region (text), or approximate GPS coordinates if you grant permission Optional
Communications Messages sent to other users through the app When used

3

How We Use Your Data

  • Provide the service — account creation, login, profile display, discovery feed, and messaging.
  • Personalise your feed — match scores are calculated using your skills, intent, and interests to surface relevant professionals.
  • Safety and trust — we use activity signals to calculate a trust score and filter out low-quality or inactive accounts from the feed.
  • Notifications — with your permission, we send push notifications for new connections and messages.
  • Improve the product — aggregated, anonymised analytics help us understand feature usage and fix bugs.
  • Legal compliance — we may process data where required by applicable law.

We do not use your personal data for advertising, sell it to third parties, or use it to train AI models.

Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored on Supabase infrastructure hosted in the European Union (AWS eu-west-1). All data is encrypted at rest (AES-256) and in transit (TLS 1.2+).

Passwords are never stored in plain text — we use bcrypt hashing with a minimum cost factor of 10.

Access to production data is restricted to authorised personnel only. We conduct periodic security reviews.

While we implement industry-standard security measures, no system is 100% secure. If you discover a vulnerability, please report it to support@buildyournetwork.online.
6

Data Retention

  • Active accounts — data is retained for as long as your account is active.
  • Deleted accounts — profile data is deleted within 30 days of account deletion. Messages may be retained for up to 90 days in backup systems before being purged.
  • Anonymised analytics — aggregated usage data with no personal identifiers may be retained indefinitely.
  • Legal holds — if data is subject to a legal obligation, it may be retained beyond these periods until the obligation is resolved.

To request early deletion of your data, email support@buildyournetwork.online with the subject line "Data Deletion Request".

Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction (including GDPR for EEA residents and CCPA for California residents), you have the following rights:

  • Access — request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correction — update inaccurate or incomplete data directly in the app, or contact us.
  • Deletion — request that we delete your personal data ("right to be forgotten").
  • Portability — receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format.
  • Objection — object to certain types of processing, including profiling.
  • Withdraw consent — where processing is based on consent, you may withdraw it at any time without affecting the lawfulness of prior processing.
  • Complaint — lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority (e.g. ICO in the UK, your national DPA in the EU).

To exercise any of these rights, email support@buildyournetwork.online. We aim to respond within 5 business days.

8

Children's Privacy

Build Your Network is intended for users aged 18 and over. We do not knowingly collect personal data from anyone under the age of 18.

If you believe a minor has created an account, please contact us immediately at support@buildyournetwork.online and we will delete the account and associated data promptly.

9

Cookies & Tracking

The BYN mobile app does not use browser cookies. The app uses AsyncStorage (local device storage) to persist your login session and consent record. This data never leaves your device to third parties.

The BYN website (buildyournetwork.online) uses minimal, essential cookies only — no advertising or tracking cookies are deployed.

We do not use Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel, or any third-party behavioural tracking on this website.
10

Push Notifications

With your permission, we send push notifications for events such as new connection requests and incoming messages. Your device push token is stored securely and used only to deliver notifications from BYN — never shared with advertisers.

You can opt out of push notifications at any time in your device's notification settings (Settings → Apps → Build Your Network → Notifications).

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we make material changes, we will:

  • Update the "Effective" date at the top of this page.
  • Send a push notification or in-app alert to active users.
  • Where required by law, request fresh consent.

Continued use of BYN after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the revised policy. If you disagree with any changes, you may delete your account at any time.
